It is an initiative by few enthusiastic college students who had the vision to do something good towards the society, to help those with whom life has been unfair. According to these students, "We cannot control the wrath of nature but we can help those who have faced it in some or the other way. We have been constantly working for the benefit of the people.Our initiative has joined hands with the NGOs who have been constantly striving for a better environment.We are really Thankful to the team of volunteers who are available for the victims of the natural calamities. They tend to help them in whichever manner they can."


The Volunteers are trying to incorporate and motivate other people as well. By seeing their enthusiasm,many people have come forward to donate money as well as clothes, food and other useful stuff.
